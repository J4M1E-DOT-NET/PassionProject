"PassionProject" is a site that gives you ideas with the click of a button.

Whether you're looking for your next reckless arts & crafts project,

inspiration for a drawing, or a new recipe to try out, this site is your

new best friend.

The site's final goal is to inspire you to start making things with your own two hands:

Learn to let your creativity out on a piece of paper every so often. Make food at home, give yourself

(and your wallet) a break from fast food. Make things to admire that you can be proud of.

Well? Get to it!
